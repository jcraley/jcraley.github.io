Jeff Craley

I am is currently a PhD student in the ECE department at JHU. I work under Archana Venkaraman in the Malone Center for Engineering in Healthcare. My research focuses on signal processing and machine learning for modeling time series medical signals.

I received my M.S. from Boston University in 2015. Before that, I earned a B.S. and B.A. from Virginia Tech in aerospace engineering and English literature, respectively, in 2011.
